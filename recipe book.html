Recipe #1

peanut butter cookies
  • Time: Under 30 mins
  • Serves: 25
Ingredients Quantity
peanut butter 1 cup/ 240g
sugar 1/2 cup/ 100g
egg 1

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 350 F (180 C)

Step 2: In a LARGE bowl mix all of the ingredients together.

Step 3: After that,scoop out a spoonful of your cookie dough and roll it into a ball. Do this to the rest of the dough and place them onto a non stick baking sheet.

Step 4: To make you cookies cook evenly, flatten the cookie balls by pressing a flat based spatular on top of them.

Step 5: Bake for 8-10 minutes or untill the bottom of the cookies are golden brown.

Step 6: Remove your cookies from the oven and take the cookies off the baking sheet to cool.

Step 7: Bon appetite!

Recipe #2

mac n cheese
  • Time: 15m
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
milk 5 cups
dry macaroni 1 lb (455g)
shredded cheddar cheese 2 cups (200g)

Step 1: in a large pot bring the milk to a boil.

Step 2: Add the pasta and stir constantly until the pasta is cooked, about 10 minutes.

Step 3: Turn off the heat, then add the cheddar. Stir until the cheese is melted and the pasta is evenly coated.

Step 4: bon appetite!

Recipe #3

mac n cheese
  • Time: 10m
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
milk 1 cup
banana 1
rolled oats 1/2 cups (50g)

Step 1: Add rolled oats to a blender and blend until the oats are the size of a fine crumb.

Step 2: Add the banana and milk and blend well.

Step 3: Pour in a glass.

Step 4: bon appetite!
